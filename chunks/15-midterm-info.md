1. Probability review, distributions
2. Convergence (in probability, in distribution), WLLN, SLLN, CLT
3. Probability inequalities (Markov, Chebyshev)
4. Delta method
5. Statistical models, estimation framework
6. Method of Moments
7. Maximum Likelihood Estimation (properties: invariance, consistency)

10. Bayesian paradigm (prior, posterior, Bayes estimators)
11. Sampling distributions (Gamma, Chi-squared, t-distribution)
